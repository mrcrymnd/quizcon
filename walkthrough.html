Before You Begin

Familiarize yourself with these unique QuizCon conventions before taking this quiz.

Making QuizCon Choices

Unlike traditional multiple choice questions, the triangle provides a range of answer possibilities.

Submitting QuizCon Answers

For each question, choose A, B, C, or anything anything in between. You can even say "I don't know."

Scoring QuizCon Points

Your score is unique calculation based on both difficulty and confidence that your instructor determines in advance.

Receiving QuizCon Results

Immediate feedback on your performance may be either immediate or released at a later date.
