Before You Begin "The Ultimate Planets Quiz"

Take a moment to understand the construction of a QuizCon question. QuizCon questions are different from traditional multiple choice questions as they allow relative choices in addition to absolute ones. Student are allowed to "guess" between choices.

Hover over each of the different components of a QuizCon question below and a popover will briefly describe how it works.

Sample Question

What planet lacks an internal magnetic field?

  1. Jupiter
  2. Mercury
  3. Venus
  4. I don't know.
