Before You Begin

Familiarize yourself with these unique QuizCon conventions before taking this quiz.

Choice Range

Unlike traditional multiple choice questions, the QuizCon triangle provides a range of answer possibilities.

Opt-out

It's not A, B, C, or anything anything in between? QuizCon lets you say "I don't know" with Option D.

Partial Credit

It's not just "correct" and "incorrect" with QuizCon. Your score is unique calculation based on both difficulty and confidence that your instructor determines in advance.
